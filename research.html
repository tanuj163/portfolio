Research Items

Multiphysics Modeling for Sensor Design

Developed a simulation framework coupling laser-induced heating and thermoelastic response for sensor applications. Enabled prediction of ultrasonic wave generation due to laser absorption, supporting defect detection and design optimization.

Deformation Mechanisms in Gold Nanoparticles

Conducted atomistic simulations to investigate the strain-rate-dependent deformation behavior of gold nanospheres and nanorods. Combined molecular dynamics with unsupervised machine learning to uncover distinct dislocation-based mechanisms.
